\subsection{Transwells setup}

Each well will be coated with collagen-IV with a final concentration of 5~$\mu g/cm^2$. The collagen-IV stock solution concentration is 1~$mg/mL$. Each well have a surface of 0,33~$cm^2$. We need \py{Q_coll_par_puit}~$\mu g$ of collagen-IV by well.


We will prepare a collagen-IV solution at \py{Cf* 1000}~$\mu g/mL$. We have \py{nb_puits} wells so we need \py{Vf} $mL$ of collagen-IV solution. We prepare the collagen-IV solution following the table \ref{table-collagen}. Then, we will add \py{vol_par_puit * 1000}~$\mu L$ of the fresh prepare solution to each well.
